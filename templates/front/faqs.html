How to add a check


To add a check, you first need to enter your preferred e-mail address on the welcome page. This will enable you to receive alerts whenever your check goes down. After the e-mail has been added, you will receive an e-mail with a login link. On clicking the link you will be redirected to the checks page and from there all you need to is to click on the add check button.

How to ping a check


To ping a check, go to the checks page and copy the ping url whenever your check goes down. There a number of ways to ping. The link can be pasted in a browser search bar and following it will return the message OK. After pinging a check it will either go from never pinged to up or down to up depending on whether it had been pinged before.
